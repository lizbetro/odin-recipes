Simple and Fool Proof Lasagna Recipe

How to make the most delicious Lasagna in 30min. The easiest recipe for beginners.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Minced Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomator Paste
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Mix and cook: ground beef, onion, garlic until brown
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water
  4. Season mixure with: sugar, basil, fennel seeds, italian seasoning, salt, pepper, parsley
  5. Let it simmer, occasionally stirring
  6. Assemble into lasagna with noodles
  7. Bake in oven for 25 minutes
  8. Enjoy
