Meatball Recipe

Recipe below is to make simple meatballs that you can throw into any food recepe that requires it

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Ground pork
  • Italian breadcrumbs
  • Milk
  • Diced Onions
  • Garlic Powder
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 Celcius
  2. Mix all the ingredients
  3. Shape mixture into 1 inch meatballs
  4. Bake 18-20 minutes or until fully cooked
